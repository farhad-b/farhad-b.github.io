Work Experience

Researcher

Oshawa, Ontario, Canada

September 2022 - December 2024

Autonomous Vehicle and Electro-Hydraulic Control Lab (AVEC-Lab)

At AVEC-Lab, I pioneered the development of the Smart Selective Navigator (SSN) using Python, tackling complex arc routing challenges with turn restrictions for autonomous vehicles. I designed SSN within an object‐oriented framework, enabling its flexible adaptation to various projects—from optimizing routes for Oshawa’s winter services to scaling for large urban scenarios in Surrey. I further developed a real-time scheduling algorithm for dynamic vehicle allocation and created an exploration algorithm using Isaac Sim, ROS, and CUDA to minimize vehicle overlap in multi-vehicle tasks.
